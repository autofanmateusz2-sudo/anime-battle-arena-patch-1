How to Play

Movement

W A S D Move   Space Jump (double jump in air)   Shift Dash (3 charges)   Mouse Camera

Combat

LMB Light Attack   RMB Heavy Attack   Q E R Abilities   F Ultimate

C Block (-70% damage)   V Parry (perfect timing reflects)   Tab Lock-on

Resources

HP - your health. MP - regenerates over time, used for abilities. ULT GAUGE - charges from damage, used for ultimate (F).

Combos

Chain hits to build combo counter. More hits = more damage scaling but caps out. Heavy attacks launch enemies into the air for juggle combos.

Tips

Each character has a unique passive — read it on the select screen! Some transform at low HP, some place markers, some have lifesteal.
